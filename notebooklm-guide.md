## STEP 2 — HOW TO UPLOAD TO NOTEBOOKLM

1. Go to **notebooklm.google.com** (free with a Google account)
2. Click **New Notebook** → name it "Murder Drones Fan Research"
3. Click **Add Source** in the left panel
4. For PDFs: click **Upload** and drag your PDF files in
5. For websites: click **Website** and paste each URL
6. For the HTML files: open each in a text editor, select all, copy, then use **Paste Text** option
7. Once all sources are added, click the **🎙️ Audio Overview** button to generate a podcast

## STEP 3 — PODCAST EPISODE PROMPTS

Paste any of these into the "Customize" box before generating your audio overview.
Each is designed to produce a focused, interesting episode.

---

### EPISODE 1 — "The Heart vs. The Rebel: N and Uzi's Story"
**Prompt:**
```
Create a podcast episode exploring the relationship between Serial Designation N and
Uzi Doorman. Discuss how they went from predator and prey to romantic partners.
Focus on what each character needed that the other provided emotionally, how their
personalities complement and clash, and what their relationship says about the show's
theme of finding connection in unexpected places. Make it conversational and passionate,
like two fans who really love these characters.
```

---

### EPISODE 2 — "The Mask and What's Underneath: Who Is V Really?"
**Prompt:**
```
Create a deep-dive podcast episode about Serial Designation V. Explore the idea that
her sadistic persona is a mask worn for survival and to protect N. Walk through her
evolution episode by episode — from "bored predator" in Episode 1 to the sacrifice in
Episode 6 to returning on a tamed Sentinel in the finale. Discuss what her character
says about trauma, identity, and found family. Include a debate: is V a hero, a villain,
or something more interesting than both?
```

---

### EPISODE 3 — "J: The Tragedy of Choosing the System"
**Prompt:**
```
Create a podcast episode defending Serial Designation J as a tragic character, not just
a villain. Explore how fear, self-preservation, and the loss of Tessa Elliott shaped her
decision to serve the Absolute Solver. Discuss what J's arc says about the dangers of
institutional loyalty over personal morality. Compare her choices to V's — both faced
the same impossible situation, but chose differently. End with whether J deserved a
better ending.
```

---

### EPISODE 4 — "The Absolute Solver Explained: From Scrap Pile to Planet-Killer"
**Prompt:**
```
Create an educational podcast episode explaining the Absolute Solver from scratch.
Start with what it is, where it came from, and how it spreads through hosts. Walk
through each host — Cyn, Nori, Yeva, Doll, and Uzi — and how the Solver behaved
differently in each. Discuss why Uzi was able to keep her identity when Cyn could not.
Make it accessible enough for someone who hasn't seen the show to understand, but
detailed enough to satisfy fans.
```

---

### EPISODE 5 — "Elliott Manor: The Night Everything Broke"
**Prompt:**
```
Create a podcast episode about the Elliott Manor backstory — the origin point for
everything in Murder Drones. Explore the relationships between young N, V, J, Cyn,
and Tessa before the series began. Discuss what the Manor massacre represents
thematically — the destruction of innocence, the moment the Solver won. Analyze how
the trauma of that night shaped each Disassembly Drone differently. Include theories
about what Tessa's full role was and whether she could have stopped what happened.
```

---

### EPISODE 6 — "Copper-9: A World Worth Saving"
**Prompt:**
```
Create a worldbuilding podcast episode about the planet Copper-9 itself. Discuss the
history of human colonization, JCJenson's corporate role, the Worker Drones' society,
and what daily life was like before and after the Disassembly Drones arrived. Explore
the question: is Copper-9 worth saving, and what does it mean that Uzi chose to save
it? Include a discussion of the show's commentary on corporate exploitation.
```

---

### EPISODE 7 — "Doll: The Saddest Character in the Show?"
**Prompt:**
```
Create a podcast episode making the case that Doll is the most tragic character in
Murder Drones. Discuss her backstory, her years living with her dead parents, her
single-minded focus on revenge against V, and the moment her motivation shifted from
revenge to trying to stop the Solver. Analyze her final message to Uzi — "Fight Back" —
and what it means that she chose to warn Uzi even knowing she was going to die. Was
her death meaningful or was it a waste?
```

---

### EPISODE 8 — "Themes Deep Dive: What Is Murder Drones Really About?"
**Prompt:**
```
Create a thematic analysis podcast episode discussing what Murder Drones is really
saying beneath the action and dark humor. Cover at least three major themes: trauma
and recovery, the danger of choosing systems over people, and the nature of identity
when something tries to erase who you are. Use specific scenes and characters as
examples. End with a discussion of why the show resonates so strongly with its audience
and what makes it stand out from other animated web series.
```

---

### EPISODE 9 — "The Best Moments: A Fan Ranking"
**Prompt:**
```
Create an enthusiastic fan podcast episode ranking and discussing the top 10 most
memorable moments in Murder Drones — across all 8 episodes. Include a mix of action
moments, emotional beats, and funny scenes. For each moment, explain why it works
so well and what it reveals about character or story. Make this feel like two people who
genuinely love the show talking about what makes it special.
```

---

### EPISODE 10 — "Unanswered Questions and Fan Theories"
**Prompt:**
```
Create a fan theory podcast episode exploring the biggest unanswered questions in
Murder Drones. Include theories about: what exactly the Absolute Solver's long-term
goals were beyond eating planets, whether Tessa could ever return in some form,
what Uzi's hybrid existence means for Copper-9's future, and whether JCJenson will
come back. For each theory, discuss evidence from the show and rate how likely
each theory is. Keep the tone fun and speculative.
```

## STEP 4 — TIPS FOR BETTER AUDIO EPISODES

✅ **Add more sources = better episodes.** NotebookLM's quality scales with source volume.

✅ **Customize before generating.** Always paste the prompt above into the Customize box
   before hitting generate. Without customization, episodes are generic summaries.

✅ **Generate multiple episodes on different days.** Each generation is slightly different —
   you can keep the best one.

✅ **For video/slideshow content:**
   - Generate the audio episode first
   - Use the NotebookLM transcript (download it)
   - Pair the transcript with your wiki HTML as chapter markers
   - Use a tool like Canva, PowerPoint, or CapCut to add visuals to the audio

✅ **GitHub Pages organization suggestion:**
```
your-repo/
├── index.html          ← Main hub (already built)
├── quiz.html           ← Personality quiz (already built)
├── wiki.html           ← Character wiki (already built)
├── podcast.html        ← Podcast episode player (coming next)
├── episodes.html       ← Episode guide (coming next)
├── audio/
│   ├── episode-01-n-and-uzi.mp3
│   ├── episode-02-who-is-v.mp3
│   └── ...
└── assets/
    ├── style.css       ← (optional shared styles)
    └── images/
```
